## Title Prompt:
- Find 5-10 accounts in your niche and filter their most popular videos.
- Copy 10-20 titles into AI and ask it to break them down into instructions on how to replicate them.
- Turn those instructions into a prompt that takes your video topic idea and generates potential titles.

## Key Points Prompt:
- Ask AI to create a guide on how to outline a YouTube video topic into compelling key points that keep the viewer engaged, ensuring the video is novel and valuable.

## Introduction Prompt:
- Find a YouTube video that teaches how to create a good video introduction.
- Turn that video's content into instructions, and then turn those instructions into a prompt.

## Script Video Prompt:
- Find a YouTube video that teaches good script creation, or find a video script you want to emulate.
- Have AI turn that into a guide.
Turn that guide into a prompt that gathers your topic, key points, and intro as context.

## B-roll Ideas Prompt:
- Ask AI for B-roll and retention best practices as instructions.
- Turn that into a prompt that adds B-roll ideas for each line of your script.
- Feed that prompt into each individual section of your script.
